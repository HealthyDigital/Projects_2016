Study design

An observational study of mucosal healing during long-term treatment with REMICADE in 214 patients with Crohn’s disease. Patients underwent an (ileo) colonoscopy prior to the start of this therapy. The primary analysis assessed what proportion of patients achieved mucosal healing during REMICADE therapy. The impact of endoscopic healing on further clinical events in these patients under continued therapy with REMICADE was investigated. Adapted from Schnitzler F et al.1
