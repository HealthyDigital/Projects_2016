Hepatosplenic T-cell lymphoma (HSTCL) and IBD1

Thirty-six cases of HSTCL in inflammatory bowel disease have been reported in the FDA
reporting system1

  • All 36 cases of HSTCL reported (from 1996 to 2009) had previous
    exposure to thiopurine1
  • No cases occurred in patients treated with anti-TNF therapy alone1
  • 20 patients received REMICADE and a thiopurine, and 16 received
    a thiopurine as monotherapy1
  • Median time from the initiation of a thiopurine to the development
    of HSTCL was 5.5 years (range 1-13.5 years)1
  • Median number of REMICADE doses was 3 (range 1-24)1
  • Of 31 patients of known gender, 29 were males1
  • Of 30 patients of known age, 27 were younger than 35 years1
