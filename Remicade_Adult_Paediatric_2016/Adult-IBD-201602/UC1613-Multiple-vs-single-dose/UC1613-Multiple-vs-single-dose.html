Study design

Kohn, 2007: Open-label, observational trial involving retrospective data for 46 patients and prospective data for 37 REMICADE patients with severe ulcerative colitis flare who failed to respond to intensive IV glucocorticoid treatment, treated with either 1, 2 or 3 infusions of REMICADE 5 mg/kg according to individual clinician preference rather than clinical response. Primary outcome was survival free from colectomy or death, within 2 months from the first REMICADE infusion. Secondary endpoint was survival free from colectomy over the long term.1
