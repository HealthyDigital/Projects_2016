Study design

A 12-week, multicentre, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial of a single infusion of REMICADE in 108 patients with moderate-to-severe Crohn’s disease that was resistant to treatment. Primary endpoint was clinical response, defined as a reduction of ≥70 points in the score of Crohn’s Disease Activity Index (CDAI) at 4 weeks that was not accompanied by a change in any concomitant medications. Clinical remission, defined as a score of <150 on the CDAI, was also evaluated.1 Adapted from Targan S et al.1
