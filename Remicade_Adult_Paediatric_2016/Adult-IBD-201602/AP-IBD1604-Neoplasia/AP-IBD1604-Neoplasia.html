Study design

A total of 6,273 North American patients with Crohn’s disease were enrolled and evaluated in an ongoing prospective, observational Crohn’s Therapy, Resource, Evaluation, and Assessment Tool (TREAT) registry (1999-2010 ), established to compare REMICADE safety with conventional nonbiological medications in Crohn’s disease.
