Non-adherence in IBD

Patient risk-factors:

In IBD and other disease, subjective
self-report may overestimate adherence1,2
  • Younger age or recent diagnosis1 Less likely to have experienced the
    consequences of non-adherence
    i.e. relapse or surgery
  • Busy working life1 Forgetfulness, short on time
  • In remission1 Less perceived need for medication,
    perception they are ‘cured’, no symptoms
    as reminder to take medication
