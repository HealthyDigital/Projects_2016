  • Acute reactions were reported in approximately 5 of 479 (1%) REMICADE infusions in 165 patients,
    not related to IgE-mediated type I hypersensitivity events1
  • Acute reactions were reported in approximately 5% of 479 REMICADE infusions in 165 patients, not related
    to IgE-mediated type I hypersensitivity events1
  • Using appropriate treatment protocols, these reactions were effectively treated and prevented upon
    retreatment in nearly all patients1
  • Delayed reactions were rare, occurring in <1% of REMICADE infusions1
