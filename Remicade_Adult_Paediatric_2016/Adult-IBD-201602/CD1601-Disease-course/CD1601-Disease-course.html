Crohn’s disease: chronic relapsing remitting condition with
cumulative structural bowel damage1

CDAI scores can be similar in both patients with recent onset CD who are naive to treatment and
in patients with a long history of CD who have extensive, irreversible bowel damage from progressive inflammation or previous surgical resection.1

The Lemann Score will measure not only current disease activity but also cumulative
structural damage to the digestive tract.1
