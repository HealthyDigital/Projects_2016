*Normal CRP, steroid-free clinical remission with mucosal healing. CD, Crohn’s disease; AZA, azathioprine; MP, mercaptopurine;
IFX, infliximab (REMICADE).
  • It is becoming accepted that an accelerated step-up approach is needed in
    patients with moderately or severely active luminal CD1
  • Earlier intervention with immunosuppressants and TNF inhibitors will induce
    and maintain remission, reduce steroid use and promote mucosal healing1
