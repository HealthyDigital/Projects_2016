REMICADE: Improved convenience with shortened infusions

Quicker maintenance treatment

Now available: One hour maintenance therapy infusions can be considered for patients who have tolerated the first
three infusions over a period of 2 hours.1#

  • Dosing regimen
  • Mode of administration IV
  • Induction dose 5 MG/KG
  • Induction dose interval 0, 2, 6 WEEKS
  • Maintenance dose 5 MG/KG
  • Maintenance dose interval EVERY 8 WEEKS
  • Tailored dosage by body weight WEIGHT-BASED DOSING
*REMICADE dosing is based on body weight.
# Shortened infusions at doses >6 mg/kg have not been studied.
Before prescribing, please refer to the REMICADE Data Sheet.
