Adherence

Adherence facts

  • People with chronic conditions only take about half of their prescribed medicine1,2
  • Rates of adherence have not changed much in the last 3 decades, despite WHO and Institute of Medicine (IOM) improvement goals2
  • Overall satisfaction with care is not typically a determining factor in medication adherence2
  • Adherence drops when there are long waiting times at clinics or long time lapses between appointments2
