Study design

ACCENT II: A multicentre, double-blind, randomised, placebo-controlled trial in 306 adult patients with Crohn’s disease and ≥1 draining peri-anal or enterocutaneous fistula of ≥3 months’ duration. Patients received 5 mg/kg IV infusion of REMICADE at weeks 0, 2 and 6 before they were randomised to 8-weekly infusions of placebo or REMICADE. Adapted from Sands B et al.1
