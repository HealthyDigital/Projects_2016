Mucosal healing improved patient outcomes in Crohn’s disease1-4

Mucosal healing was associated with a reduced need for:

  • Corticosteroids1
  • Crohn’s disease-related hospitalisations2
  • Surgical resections and major
    abdominal surgeries1,3

treatment
of [Crohn’s
disease] should
therefore
extend beyond
symptomatic
remission
to include
mucosal
healing, as this
endpoint has
been shown to
predict longterm
outcome.”4
