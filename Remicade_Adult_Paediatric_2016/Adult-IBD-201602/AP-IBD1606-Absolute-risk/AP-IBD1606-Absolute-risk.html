Hepatosplenic T-cell lymphoma (HSTCL) risk1

Absolute risk estimate for factors associated with HSTCL in inflammatory bowel disease patients:1

  • Thiopurine use: 1 in 45,000 chance1
  • Anti-TNF therapy + thiopurine: <1 in 22,0001
  • Male + thiopurine + <35 years: 1 in 7,4041
  • Male + thiopurine + <35 years + anti-TNF therapy: 1 in 3,5341
