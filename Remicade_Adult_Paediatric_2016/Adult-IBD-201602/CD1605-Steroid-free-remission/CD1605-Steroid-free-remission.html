Study design

ACCENT 1: 46-week, multi-centre, double-blind trial of 573 patients with Crohn’s disease (CDAI >220) treated with REMICADE 5 mg/kg at week 0, then randomly assigned to receive infusions at weeks 2 & 6 and then 8 weekly of either placebo (group I) or REMICADE 5 mg/kg (group II) or REMICADE 5 mg/kg at weeks 2 & 6, followed by 10 mg/kg 8-weekly (group III). Primary endpoints were the time to loss of response (of week 2 responders) up to week 54 and the proportion of week 2 responders in remission (CDAI <150) at week 30. Adapted from Hanauer et al.1

SONIC: 50-week (30 week with blinded 20 week extension), multi-centre, double-blind trial of 508 patients with moderate-to-severe Crohn’s disease (CDAI >220) randomised to receive either REMICADE 5 mg/kg at week 0, 2 & 6, then 8 weekly plus daily oral placebo, 2.5 mg/kg oral azathioprine plus placebo infusions on the standard schedule, or both active treatments combined. Primary endpoint was steroid-free remission (CDAI <150). Adapted from Colombel et al.2
