REMICADE may reduce hospitalisations and surgeries1

Fistulising Crohn’s disease-related hospitalisations and surgeries in REMICADE responders1

Adapted from Lichtenstein G et al, 20051
  • REMICADE maintenance treatment offers:
    -  >50% ↓ in mean no. of Crohn’s disease-related hospitalisations vs. placebo1
    -  >70% ↓ in mean no. of Crohn’s disease-related inpatient surgeries, procedures and major surgeries1
    -  50% ↓ in all surgeries and procedures1

Study design

282 patients (from ACCENT II) were randomised at week 24 as responders or non-responders to receive placebo or 5 mg/kg REMICADE maintenance every 8 weeks following induction therapy. Data on Crohn’s disease-related hospitalisations, surgeries, and procedures were compared between the treatment groups for responders and all randomised patients.Adapted from Lichtenstein G et al.1
