REMICADE provided clinically meaningful improvement in the health-related quality of life (QoL) of patients with ulcerative colitis as early as Week 8 and this was sustained out to 1 year.1

Study design

Feagan, 2007: In two placebo-controlled, double-blind studies (the Active Ulcerative Colitis Trials 1 and 2 [ACT-1 and -2]), 728 patients were randomised to placebo or REMICADE. Infusions were administered at weeks 0, 2, 6, and every 8 weeks thereafter, up to week 22 (ACT-2) or 46 (ACT-1). Changes in IBDQ and SF-36 (PCS and MCS, respectively) scores were analysed.1
