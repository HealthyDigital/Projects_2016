Study design

Monterubbianesi, 2014: Observational study of 113 patients hospitalised with acute severe ulcerative colitis, refractory to IV methylperdnisolone 40-60 mg daily for >7 days. Patients received REMICADE 5 mg/kg at weeks 0, 2 and 6. Aminosalicylates, thiopurines or scheduled REMICADE (5 mg/kg 8-weekly) were allowed as maintenance treatment. Co-primary endpoints were colectomy within 3 months and 1 year from the first REMICADE infusion. Secondary endpoints were overall colectomyfree survival and the identification of possible predictive factors of colectomy.1
