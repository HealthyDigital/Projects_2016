Study design

Ferrante, 2008: Follow up study of 121 consecutive patients with ulcerative colitis uncontrolled by conventional therapy, treated with either: a single dose of REMICADE 5 or 10 mg/kg or induction schedule (5 or 10 mg/kg at weeks 0, 2 and 6). Short term response and mucosal healing (defined as Mayo endoscopy subscore of 0 or 1 and only assessed in patients with Mayo subscore of >2 at inclusion) were assessed at wk 4 in patients who received a single infusion, wk 10 for those who received the full induction course. Responders to REMICADE therapy (induction or single dose) could receive either maintenance with REMICADE 5 or 10 mg/kg every 8 weeks (75% of patients) or episodic therapy (25% of patients). Colectomy-free survival was assessed over a median follow-up period of 33 (17-49.8) months.1
