Study design

Retrospective review of medical records of patients with fistulising Crohn’s disease treated with REMICADE (n=156) at 2 French referral centres (between 1998-2011). Events assessed included fistula closure (dened as the absence of any draining by fistula openings at one visit), and sustained fistula closure for patients without any recurrence during the follow up after perianal Crohn’s disease healing. Median follow up was 5 years. Adapted from Bouguen G et al.1
