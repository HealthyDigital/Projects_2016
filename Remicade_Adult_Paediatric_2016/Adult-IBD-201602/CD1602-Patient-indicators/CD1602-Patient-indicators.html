For which patient is early intervention needed
in Crohn’s disease?1-3

The following prognostic indicators increase the likelihood of rapid progression in Crohn’s disease:

  • Ileo-colonic or small bowel disease location1,2
  • Young age of onset <40 years2
  • Perianal disease1,2
  • Need for steroids for the first flare1,2
