Adherence to REMICADE in Crohn’s disease patients
with durable response was favoured over switching to
adalimumab (Humira®†)1

Treatment Cessation1

† Humira is a registered trademark of Abbvie New Zealand.
  • Fewer REMICADE-treated patients stopped therapy due to loss of response
    or intolerance vs. patients who switched to Humira®† (2% vs. 28%, p<0.01)1
  • Almost a third of patients who switched to Humira®† returned to REMICADE
    therapy within a year1
“Elective switching to [Humira®] leads to worse outcomes than maintaining treatment with [REMICADE].”1

Study design

SWITCH: An open-label randomised single-centre trial of 73 patients with ongoing response to ≥ 6 months of scheduled maintenance REMICADE. Patients were randomised to continue REMICADE or to switch to adalimumab for 1 year. Dose optimisation was allowed for intermittent flares, and patients with loss of response or intolerance could cross over to the alternative treatment group. Tolerability, patient preference and efficacy lof both treatment options were the primary outcomes. Adapted from van Assche et al.1
