Study design

Prospective survey of 100 anti-TNF naïve patients with Crohn’s disease. Prior to the completion of a survey to explore medication preferences, patients were provided with a written description of the three anti-TNF agents, focusing on indications, mode of administration, side effects, and scientific evidence of efficacy and safety for each drug. NOTE: certolizumab pegol data not included as this drug is not registered or funded in New Zealand.
