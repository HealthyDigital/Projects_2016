Study design

REACH: 112 paediatric patients with moderately to severely active Crohn’s disease and a Paediatric Crohn’s Disease Activity Index (PCDAI) score >30 received REMICADE 5 mg/kg at weeks 0, 2 and 6. Patients responding to treatment at week 10 were randomised to REMICADE 5 mg/kg every 8 or 12 weeks through week 46. A concurrent immunomodulator was required. Clinical response and clinical remission were evaluated at weeks 10, 30, and 54. Patients who completed treatment through week 46, including those who crossed over, and in the opinion of the study investigator would benefit from continued treatment, were eligible to enter the REACH open-label extension (OLE) to allow uninterrupted access to REMICADE therapy. Sixty children entered the OLE: 33, 12, and 15 patients were receiving REMICADE 5 mg/kg every 8 weeks, 5 mg/kg every 12 weeks, and 10 mg/kg every 8 weeks, respectively, at extension entry.
