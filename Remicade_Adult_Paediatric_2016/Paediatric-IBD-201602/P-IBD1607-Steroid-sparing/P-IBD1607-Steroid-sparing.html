Study design

Hyams, 2010: A multicentre cohort study of 332 paediatric patients with ulcerative colitis enrolled in the Paediatric Inflammatory Bowel Disease Collaborative Research Group Registry. Children ≤16 years of age and newly diagnosed with ulcerative colitis were enrolled in the registry. Of 332 patients, 52 (16%) received REMICADE. Corticosteroid-free inactive disease was observed in 38% and 21% of patients at 12 and 24 months, respectively. By 24 months, 61% of patients had avoided colectomy.1
