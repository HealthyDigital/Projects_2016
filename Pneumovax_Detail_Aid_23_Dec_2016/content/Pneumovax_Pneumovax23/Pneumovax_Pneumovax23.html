ALL ADULTS 65 YEARS AND OVER5#

Revaccination is funded under the NIP for
all patients with the following risk factors:3

  • Diabetes
  • Chronic lung,
    heart, kidney
    and liver disease
  • Impaired immunity
  • Alcohol related
    problems
  • Down Syndrome
  • Cochlear implants
  • Asplenia
  • Intracranial shunts
  • Cerebrospinal
    fluid leak
  • Tobacco smokers
