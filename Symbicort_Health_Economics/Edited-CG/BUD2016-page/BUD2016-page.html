Burden of disease - COPD in Australia1

In 2012, 5,923 deaths from COPD (3,292 males and 2,631 females)
making it the 5th leading cause of death1

Direct health expenditure attributed to COPD in Australia estimated
~$929 million1

  • Admitted patient costs(534M)
  • Out-of-hospital medical services(181M)
  • Prescription medicines(214M)
  • Major cost burden on hospitals

~59,680 hospitalisations of people aged 55+ years where COPD was the principal diagnosis. The rate of hospitalisation for COPD among those aged 55+ ~1,052 per 100,000 population1

Reference: 1. Australian Institute of Health & Welfare, 2012. Available at www.aihw.gov.au/copd/
