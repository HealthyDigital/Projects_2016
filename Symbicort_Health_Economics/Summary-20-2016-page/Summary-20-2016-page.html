Summary

  • COPD is a major cost burden for the Australian healthcare system and hospitals1

  • Symbicort provides a unit cost benefit through
    use of the Rapihaler over the Seretide pMDI

  • Symbicort has shown significant reduction in exacerbations
    vs. Seretide in real-world evidence2

  • Patients save on Symbicort Rapihaler

References: 1. Australian Institute of Health & Welfare, 2012. Available at www.aihw.gov.au/copd/ 2. Larsson K et al. J Intern Med 2013; 273:584-594.
