PATHOS study1

  • R
  • S

A population-based, retrospective observational registry study linking data
from primary care medical records and national registries in Sweden:

  • 9,893 patients up to 11 years follow up

  • 2 matched cohorts of 2,734 patients each with similar characteristics were compared

  • No formal stratification of primary healthcare centres but effor made to ensure that the sample was representative of the Swedish population
    (i.e. mixture rural/urban, private /public small/large centre size)

  • Data based on 'real-word' evidence (increasingly sought after by PBAC)

Limitations: Unknown confounding factors and data is limited to the variables recorded. COPD diagnosis accuracy and severity could not be fully verified. Medication
use is based on prescriptions not actual use. Nevertheless, as the study is based on a large sample (almost 5500 individuals), it is not likely that potential confounding
factors would differ between the cohorts.

Reference: 1. Larsson K et al. J Intern Med 2013; 273:584-594.
