There is a strong seasonal driver of COPD exacerbations1

Admissions to hospital for COPD are highest in winter and early spring.
This matches and is related to the trend for acute respiratory infections
(e.g. rhinovirus (common cold), influenza, pneumonia and acute bronchitis).1

Reference: 1. Australian Institute of Health & Welfare, 2014. Available at www.aihw.gov.au/copd/
