Significant reduction in exacerbations*
and hospitalisations when Symbicort is added to Spiriva®1

*Exacerbations defined as worsening of COPD requiring use of systemic steroids (oral or parenteral) and/or hospitalisation.
Secondary endpoint, primary endpoint was met. 12 week study.

  • R
  • S

Adapted from Welte et al. 2009.1 A 12-week, randomised, double-blind, parallel-group study in 660 patients (≥40 yrs) with moderate-severe COPD (FEV1 ≤50% predicted normal and a history of exacerbations requiring systemic steroids and/or antibiotics).
Reduction in rate of exacerbations and hospitalisations at 12 weeks; Symbicort 400/12 bd + tiotropium 18µg od vs. tiotropium 18µg od + placebo.
Reference: 1. Welte T et al. Am J Respir Crit Care Med 2009; 180:741–750.
