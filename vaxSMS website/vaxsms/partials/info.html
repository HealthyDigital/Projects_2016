About this website

This is a RotaTeq Dosing Interval Aid.

This is a smart device based websitelication designed to assist Australian healthcare professionals in establishing immunisation cut-off dates for RotaTeq.

Please note that the Dosing Interval Aid is a guide only to assist with identifying the range of dates for the administration of the 1st, 2nd and 3rd dose of RotaTeq, following a specific birth date.

It will also calculate the 6 week, 4 month and 6 month immunisation dates based on the birthdate. Please note that the actual dates for immunisation may vary from the calculated dates depending on the date of the childâ€™s last immunisation and other circumstances. Please always refer to the Australian Immunisation Handbook and relevant Websiteroved Product Information when establishing immunisation dates.

This website is intended for Australian healthcare professional use only.
