WHO'S MISSING OUT?

Those eligible for vaccination under
the National Immunisation Program

  • Boys and girls aged 12–13 years of
    age are eligible for free HPV vaccine
    as part of the school-based National
    Immunisation Program (NIP)1
  • Up to 80% of Australian females
    aged 12 to 26 were vaccinated under
    the government funded National
    HPV Vaccination Program between
    2007–20092

Have your patients had all 3 doses?

For men up to 26 years and women
up to 45 years, HPV vaccination is not
Government funded, but is available
as a private prescription.

Consider patients presenting for:

  • Sexual Health Consultation
  • Travel Vaccinations
  • Routine Health Check Up
  • Routine Pap smear
  • Oral Contraceptive pill script

Overseas students

  • Many international students
    were not eligible for
    vaccination under the National
    Immunisation Program
  • Due to inadequate knowledge
    of risks, consequences and
    protection mechanisms, the
    international student population
    may be at higher risk of sexual
    health problems3,4

Consider patients presenting for:

  • Sexual health consultation
  • Travel vaccinations
  • Routine health check-up
  • Routine Pap smear
  • Oral contraceptive pill script
