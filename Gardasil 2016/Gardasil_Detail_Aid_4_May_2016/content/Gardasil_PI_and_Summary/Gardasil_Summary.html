  • women up to 45

    GARDASIL is indicated in females aged 9 through 45* years for the
    prevention of cervical, vulvar, vaginal and anal cancer, precancerous
    or dysplastic lesions, genital warts, and infection caused by HPV
    6/11/16/18.1

  • Men up to 26

    GARDASIL is indicated in males aged 9 through 26* years for the
    prevention of anal cancer, precancerous or dysplastic lesions,
    external genital lesions and infection caused by HPV 6/11/16/18.1

  • *Immunogenicity studies have been conducted to link efficacy in females and
     males aged 16 to 26 years to the younger populations.
