tredmaxx

non slip safety

About Intac

Tredmaxx Non-Slip Safety delivers non-slip surfacing and stair nosing for internal or external stairs, walkways and platforms.

Used as a heavy duty safety measure to secure firm footing while highlighting the edge of the step, Tredmaxx products are manufactured from industrial grade fibre-reinforced plastic (FRP) or marine grade aluminium with a silicon oxide gritted wear and corrosion resistant surface.
Supplied cut to length for installation on any substrate, Tredmaxx products can be easily installed with adhesives and / or mechanical fixings.

Applications

  • Service platforms and mezzanines
  • Warehouses and loading bays
  • Airports and shopping centres
  • Pedestrian pathways and footbridges
  • Community centres and carparks
  • Firestairs

Compliance

  • Tredmaxx products have achieved the highest slip resistance classification V - "very low risk" of slipping when wet.
  • Tredmaxx products have been wet pendulum and dry floor friction tested in accordance with Australian Standard AS/NZS 4586:2004 – Slip Resistance Classification of New Pedestrian Surface Materials.
