About
Bikesafe

Bikesafe Bikeway Barriers are designed to provide the ultimate protection for pedestrians and cyclists travelling at speed.

Pre-engineered, preconfigured and independently load tested for compliance with Austroad’s safety requirements for bikeways and paths.

Bikesafe Bikeway Barriers are manufactured from hot dip galvanized steel with flush connection points for a smoother, safer finish.

Applications

  • Cycle paths and bikeways
  • Shared pedestrian paths
  • Protection over culverts
  • Footbridges

Extract from Austroads Guide To Road Design; Part 6A 7.2.2

  • The installation of a fence at the side of a path used by cyclists is desirable where:
  • there is a steep batter or large vertical drop located in close proximity to the path
  • • the path is adjacent to an arterial road and it is necessary to restrict cyclist access to the road
  • • a bridge or culvert exists on a path •a hazard exists adjacent to a particular bicycle facility
  • • cyclists are likely to be ‘blazing a separate trail’ at an intersection between paths or around a path terminal.
