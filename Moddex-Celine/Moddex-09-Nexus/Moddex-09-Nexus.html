About
Nexus

Nexus Perimeter Barriers and Bollards create boundaries across sports and recreation fields, public parks and carparks. Preconfigured to cater for 30 plus metre radiuses or long straight distances.

Nexus Perimeter Barriers and Bollards are pre-engineered for rakes of up to four degrees in either direction without custom rolling or bending.

Manufactured from galvanized, powdercoated steel with custom colours and logo options for architectural styling available on request.

Applications

  • Sports and recreation fields
  • Public parks and ovals
  • Carparks and sightseeing
