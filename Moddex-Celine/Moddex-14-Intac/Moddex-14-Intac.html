About Intac

Intac Tactile Indicators provide walkway or directional guidance for the blind or visually impaired to establish an early warning of impending hazards.

For application across all public walkways, including stairs, ramps, escalators and railway platforms, Intac Tactile Indicators are manufactured from ceramic tile or UV resistant PVC, with self-adhesive backing for immediate installation.

Suitable for interior or exterior applications with a wide colour range, Intac Tactile Indicators can satisfy the 30% luminance contrast requirements of Australian Standard AS/NZS 1428.4.1:2009

Applications

  • DDA access ramps and stairs
  • Public walkways and thoroughfares
  • Railway platforms and traffic crossings
  • Escalators, shopping centres & carparks
  • Sports and recreation centres
