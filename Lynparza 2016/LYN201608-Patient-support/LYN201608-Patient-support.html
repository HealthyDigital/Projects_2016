  • PATIENT SUPPORT LYNPARZA PATIENT ACCESS PROGRAMME

    • PATIENT ASSESSMENT

      Patient assessed by doctor and identified
      as suitable for LYNPARZA

    • PATIENT REGISTRATION

      Doctor completes online registration form via
      www.astrazenecaoncology.com.au + writes a private script for patient
      Doctor provides patient with CMI

    • PRODUCT SUPPLY TO PHARMACY

      ZEST Healthcare enrols the patient in
      the PAP and delivers LYNPARZA to the
      nominated pharmacy

    • PATIENT CONTINUATION

      Doctor completes online continuation form via
      www.astrazenecaoncology.com.au + writes a private script for patient

    • PATIENT PICK UP

      Patient takes private script to
      pharmacy and picks up LYNPARZA   (Patient will pay at Month 3 & 6)

    • Patient receives SMS (or phone call) notification that script is ready for collection
