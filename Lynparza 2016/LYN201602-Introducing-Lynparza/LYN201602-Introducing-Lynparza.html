Introducing LYNPARZA™

A first-in-class PARP inhibitor (PARPi) that allows
women with BRCA mutated (BRCAm) platinum-sensitive
relapsed High grade serous ovarian cancer to extend
progression-free survival (PFS).*1-4

*as maintenance therapy for PSR disease, in response after
  platinum-based chemotherapy (must have ≥2 courses)
