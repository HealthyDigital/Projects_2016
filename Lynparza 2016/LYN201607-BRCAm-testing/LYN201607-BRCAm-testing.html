    LYNPARZA is the only oral TGA approved maintenance
    therapy for BRCAm platinum-sensitive relapsed high-grade
    serous ovarian cancer patients4

    • The recommended dose of Lynparza is 400 mg
      (eight 50mg capsules) taken twice daily, equivalent
      to a total daily dose of 800 mg.4
    • Patients should start treatment with Lynparza
      no later than 8 weeks after completion of their
      final dose of the platinum containing regimen.4
    • It is recommended that treatment be continued
      until progression of the underlying disease. There
      are no data to support retreatment with Lynparza
      as maintenance following subsequent relapse.4

    MISSING DOSE

    • If a patient misses a dose of Lynparza, they should
      take their next normal dose at its scheduled time.
