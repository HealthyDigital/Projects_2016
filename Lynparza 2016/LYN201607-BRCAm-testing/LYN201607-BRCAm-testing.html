    HGSOC=High Grade Serous Ovarian Cancer * Lynparza is indicated as monotherapy for the maintenance treatment of adult patients with platinum-sensitive
    relapsed BRCA-mutated (germline and/or somantic) high grade serous epithelial ovarian, fallopian tube, or primary
    peritoneal cancer who are in response (complete response or partial response) to platinum-based chemotherapy.4
    † Platinum-sensitive defined by a relapse-free period of ≥6 months following a respose to the final dose of
    platinum-based chemotherapy treatment.

    During the patient consultation:

    1. Contact the genetic counselling hotline on 1800 770 735 to arrange a pre-test, telephone based
      genetic counselling appointment.
    2. Genetic counselling service provides patient with a testing kit containing the required documentation
      and collection materials.
    3. Patient attends local pathology service for blood collection and kit is sent to testing laboratory
      in supplied pre-paid self-addressed satchel.

    BRCA mutation testing results and interpretation are sent to the referring clinician and also sent
    to PMCC genetic counselling service.
