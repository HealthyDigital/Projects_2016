• NHMRC recommends for travellers at increased risk of
    diarrhoeal disease visiting areas
    epidemic or endemic for cholera2

    This includes travellers with:

    • Inflammatory bowel disease
    • Poorly controlled or
      complicated diabetes
    • Impaired immunity
    • Significant
      cardiovascular disease
    • Achlorhydria

  • Other travellers to, or acquiring cholera1,7

    • Visiting friends and relatives
      in an endemic country
    • Insufficient access to medical
      facilities such as backpackers
      visiting remote areas
    • Long term travellers
      to endemic areas
    • Patients taking
      medication that
      lowers gastric acidity
