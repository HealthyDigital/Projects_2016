PNEUMOCOCCAL PNEUMONIA FACTS

  • Pneumonia and Influenza are leading causes of vaccine-preventable deaths amongst older adults.7-9
  • Ageing populations and increasing numbers of people with chronic
    disease are worsening the burden of pneumococcal disease.9
  • As many as 44% of Australians aged 65 to 74 cite the reason
    for not having the first vaccination is because their doctor has
    not recommended it.10
  • Pneumococcal polysaccharide vaccines (PPVs) have demonstrated
    74% efficacy against invasive pneumococcal disease.9*
*Cochrane Collaboration meta-analysis of 18 RCTS of PPVs of 2–23 valencies,
including 10 PPV23 studies (OR 0.26, 95% CI 0.14-0.45).
