AstraZeneca

Has been at the forefront of breast cancer treatment development for more than 40 years1, starting
with the discovery of NOLVADEX (tamoxifen).

NOLVADEX has been providing effective endocrine therapy to treat ER+* breast cancer for
close to 40 years.2 The arrival of NOLVADEX was an important step towards the development of
third-generation aromatase inhibitors such as ARIMIDEX (anastrozole) and the SERM FASLODEX
(fulvestrant).2

To this day we continue our dedication to active research programs in breast cancer3 and maintain
our ongoing commitment to patient support.

*Oestrogen receptor positive
